Curacao Neighbourhoods Map

Average income per neighbourhood

Description

This map shows a measurement of population per unit area of the neighbourhoods. It also shows the number of households per unit area of the neighbourhoods in Curacao. The map shows the income in each neighbourhood when you check the checkbox.
